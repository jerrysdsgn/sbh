Webflow FAQs

Can I use Webflow for free?

Yes. However, your website will be referenced to a Webflow domain (e.g. yoursite.webflow.com), and when you are using it for free you have limited access to several features

Is Webflow hard?

There IS a learning curve the first couple times you pick it up (watch: Webflow 101 video). It soon becomes easy and intuitive to use, offering a high degree of customization and depth. At the end of the day, very worth it.

What can you build with Webflow?

You can build anything from simple landing pages, to multi-page websites, CMS and blogging websites, and online stores

Does Webflow need web hosting?

No, the platform has its prebuilt hosting service.
