FAQs

How to implement SEO on Wix

You can do it through the Wix SEO Wiz. On you account dashboard, go to the SEO section and in there set your keywords to index your website

Can I use an existing domain on a Wix website?

Yes. You can transfer your domain name to your Wix account and then use it on your website

Can Wix host my website?

Yes, Wix has an integrated web hosting service

Can I pay monthly?

Yes, you can choose between two payment options; either billed monthly and billed annually

Can you edit your site after publishing?

Yes. You can edit your site at any time. In your dashboard, go to the Manage Site option and then access Edit Site
